### BHP/ (10 files)
BHP contractor management framework — forms, checklists, KPI guidelines, mobilisation checklists. Enterprise-level PC requirements.

### CMP/ (28 files)
Complete construction project management plan with full form set (F00–F27). Covers: toolbox records, induction, daily risk assessment, hazardous chemicals, subcontractors, incidents, training, plant, electrical, injuries, hazard reports, inspections, SWMS, environmental, non-conformance, corrective actions, waste, hold & witness points. Good benchmark for Breadcrumb module configuration.

### WDA/ (90+ files)
Complete WDA (Waterway Design Authority) CEHS management system. Comprehensive set covering:
- **Management plan:** WDA-CEHS-PLN-001 Rev 7
- **Checklists (C001–C008):** Bomb threat, excavation risk, safety hazard ID, weekly assessment, works to proceed, CEHS assessment, legal requirements, SWMS review
- **Safe work procedures (D100–D147):** 48 activity-specific procedures covering plant, hand tools, power tools, compressed air, explosive-powered tools, oxy-acetylene, ladders, scaffolding, lifting, welding, electrical, cranes, vehicles, workbox, demolition, underground services, overhead power lines, working at heights, water, traffic, manual handling, PPE, asbestos, hazardous substances, and more
- **Forms (F001–F042):** Permits (sewer, confined space, hot works, excavation, harness), incident reports, hazard reports, toolbox records, induction, rehabilitation/RTW
- **Flowcharts (FLO001–FLO004):** Incident communication, management system, injury reporting, unexpected asbestos
- **Procedures (PRO001–PRO016):** Fitness for work, incident management, marine emergency, confined space, isolation, rehabilitation
- **Registers (R001–R013):** Hazmat, plant, injury, visitors, height safety, SWMS, electrical, incidents
